# Collected wiring information

[Xtronics Wiki wiring info](https://xtronics.com/wiki/Thermostat_signals_and_wiring.html)


# (initial) Supported wiring
* C - Common 24v ground
* R - 24v positive
* G - Fan on
* W - Heat on
* Y - Cool on

# Easy additions
* Rh - 24v positive to enable heat
* Rc - 24v positive to enable cooling
